Terms & Privacy — draft

This is a placeholder draft, not reviewed by a lawyer. Replace before real launch.

What Backbond stores: your email, profile details you provide, posts, comments, likes, circle and plan activity, photos you upload to Moments, and derived bond scores between you and people you interact with.

Who can see what: posts and comments are visible to any signed-in user. Moments photos are visible only to people who actually attended the same plan. Bond scores are visible only to the two people involved.

Your rights: you can export your data or delete it at any time from the Safety screen. Deleting removes your profile and everything linked to it from the database.

Reporting & safety: reports you submit are reviewed by an admin. We can remove content or restrict accounts that violate house rules.
