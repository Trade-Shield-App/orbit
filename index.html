Safety & culture

To make friends with strangers, the product has to prevent creepiness, harassment and dating-app drift.

🔒

DMs unlock later

No instant stranger inboxing. Public or circle interaction comes first.

👥

Circles before cold chats

The safest route to friendship is group familiarity before one-to-one access.

🧭

Culture by design

Rank comments, kindness, repeated interaction and circle health above thirst or clout.

Non-negotiable product rules

No follower-count obsession

Visibility matters, but friendships and circles are the real status layer.

Anti-dating drift

DM limits, circle-first discovery, relationship intent filters, and behaviour moderation.

Trust signals

Phone verification, report/block, account age, comment limits and stronger controls for vulnerable users.

Memories over endless scrolling

The product rewards people for building moments with others, not just consuming content forever.
